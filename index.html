Stuff about me etc etc

Shanghai

I was born in Shanghai and spent the first half of my childhood in 1990’s pre-modernized China. As a native speaker of Chinese, I still have many connections in China even after all these years.

Kyoto

My family moved to Kyoto towards the late 90’s, where I spent the second half of my childhood. Because I received my grade school education in Japan, I am a native speaker of Japanese as well.

Hook Em' Horns

After finishing up Middle and High school in Houston, I spent the next four years of my life at University of Texas at Austin. I attended McCombs School of Business with major in Management Information System and minor in Accounting. I also studied abroad at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

Creativity!

While studying at UT Austin, I was able to dive into the world of professional web designing by working as a freelance web designer under my company - Austin Pinnacle Web Studios. While I mostly worked with various individual clients, I also had the pleasure to work with for couple start-up companies in Austin.

IT World

Upon finishing my degree at UT Austin, I jumpstarted my post-college life by joining United Services Automobile Association at San Antonio as a Software Developer. I’m currently working in a team oriented environment with very bright and wonderful coworkers (especially my boss!).
