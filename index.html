More about Esheng

Yixin is a creative individual who's always asking the question 'why' rather than 'how'. Yixin got involved in the world of web programming 8 years ago, and is currently working full time at USAA. Besides web programming, Esheng is also a metalhead guitarist, passionate cook, food junkie, and avid manga reader!

Shanghai

Esheng was born in Shanghai and spent the first half of his childhood in 1990’s pre-modernized China. As a native speaker of Chinese, Esheng somehow still have connections in China even after all the years.

Kyoto

Esheng's family moved to Kyoto around late 90’s, and lived in Japan for 3 years. He's a native speaker of Japanese as well as Chinese.

Hook Em' Horns

After finishing up Middle and High school in Houston, Esheng spent the next four years of his life at University of Texas at Austin. Esheng attended McCombs School of Business with major in Management Information System and minor in Accounting. He also studied abroad at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

Creativity!

While studying at UT Austin, Esheng started his professional career in web programming by working as a freelance web designer. While he mostly worked with individual clients, he also had the opportunity to work for couple start-up companies in Austin.

IT World

Upon finishing his bachelor's degree, Esheng became an employee at United Services Automobile Association at San Antonio as a Software Developer. He's currently working in a team oriented environment with other bright and wonderful coworkers!
